# Chest X-ray (CXR) — clinical context (India)

- **TB endemicity:** interpret opacities and lymphadenopathy with local TB prevalence; consider GeneXpert / sputum workup per national guidelines when clinically indicated.
- **Occupational lung disease:** silicosis, coal workers’ pneumoconiosis in mining, construction, stone crushing — correlate exposure history.
- **RHD / CHD:** cardiomegaly and chamber patterns in younger patients — echo referral when scores support structural disease.
- **Air pollution / biomass smoke:** chronic interstitial patterns vs acute infection.
